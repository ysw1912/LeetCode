
### 70. Climbing Stairs

-----

&emsp;&emsp;爬梯子，需要`n`步到达梯子顶，每次可爬 1 或 2 步，返回有多少方式可爬到梯子顶端。

**Example 1**

    Input: 2
    Output: 2
    Explanation: There are two ways to climb to the top.
    1. 1 step + 1 step
    2. 2 steps
    
**Example 2**


    Input: 3
    Output: 3
    Explanation: There are three ways to climb to the top.
    1. 1 step + 1 step + 1 step
    2. 1 step + 2 steps
    3. 2 steps + 1 step
